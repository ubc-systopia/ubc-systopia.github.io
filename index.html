How It Works

Pledge

Groups of friends form a voting team. Each member makes a commitment to vote in an upcoming election. In some games, players back their commitment up with a monetary pledge: Players are ONLY charged their pledges if one or more members of their team doesn’t vote.

Nudge

Once teams are formed, motivote gamifies the process of preparing to vote: Our tailored reminders and a customized roadmap set you up for success. With built-in tools to stay on track as you face off against other teams, motivote makes the entire process through fun, easy and social.

Vote

Show your friends (and motivote) that you voted by taking a photo sporting your “I voted” sticker (or with your voting receipt) outside of your polling location. Teams win when everyone on their team votes.
